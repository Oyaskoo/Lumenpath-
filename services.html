Our Therapy Services

We offer a safe and non-judgmental space to explore your challenges. Our approach is tailored to your unique needs, helping you find a path forward.

Individual Therapy

Individual therapy is a collaborative process between you and your therapist. It provides a confidential environment where you can explore your feelings, beliefs, and behaviors.

We help you work through challenging or influential memories, identify aspects of your life that you would like to change, better understand yourself and others, set personal goals, and work toward desired change.

  • Anxiety & Stress Management
  • Depression & Mood Disorders
  • Life Transitions
  • Self-Esteem & Identity

Couples Therapy

Relationships are complex. Couples therapy helps partners improve communication, resolve conflict, and strengthen their connection.

Whether you are looking to deepen your bond, navigate a crisis, or improve your day-to-day interactions, we provide the tools and guidance needed to foster a healthy relationship.

  • Communication Issues
  • Conflict Resolution
  • Intimacy & Connection
  • Pre-marital Counseling

Trauma & Healing

Trauma can leave deep imprints on our minds and bodies. We use evidence-based approaches like EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) to help you process traumatic experiences.

Our goal is to help you move past the pain of the past and reclaim your life in the present, fostering resilience and post-traumatic growth.

  • PTSD & C-PTSD
  • Childhood Trauma
  • Emotional regulation
  • Somatic experiencing
