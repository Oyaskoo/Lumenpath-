About Lumenpath

Registered Psychotherapist

I founded Lumenpath Psychotherapy with a mission to create a safe, inclusive, and transformative space for individuals seeking healing and growth.

My work is grounded in evidence-based approaches, including EMDR therapy and Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), and informed by advanced clinical training in psychiatric settings.
