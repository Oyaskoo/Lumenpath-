About Lumenpath

Understanding your story, strengthening your inner resources.

Hello, I'm Meekha Anna Saji

Registered Psychotherapist

Welcome to Lumenpath Psychotherapy. My mission is to provide a warm, inclusive, and transformative space where you can explore your inner world and find your path to healing.

With a compassionate, culturally sensitive, and trauma-informed approach, I work with individuals, couples, and families. Whether you are dealing with anxiety, depression, relationship struggles, or the effects of past trauma, I am here to support you.

Healing is not about "fixing" you—it's about understanding your story, honouring your experiences, and empowering you to live a life that feels authentic and meaningful.

Qualifications & Focus Areas

  • Registered Psychotherapist (CRPO)
  • Specialization in Trauma & Anxiety
  • Couples & Family Therapy
  • Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) & Mindfulness
  • Culturally Sensitive Care

My Therapeutic Approach

Client-Centered

You are the expert on your own life. I provide guidance and tools, but our work is collaborative and tailored to your unique goals.

Trauma-Informed

I prioritize safety, trust, and empowerment, recognizing the impact of past experiences on present well-being.

Holistic

We explore not just symptoms, but the whole person—emotional, mental, relational, and physical aspects of health.
