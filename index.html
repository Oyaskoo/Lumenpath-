Hello, I'm Meekha Anna Saji

Registered Psychotherapist

Welcome to Lumenpath Psychotherapy. My mission is to provide a warm, inclusive, and transformative space where you can explore your inner world and find your path to healing.

With a compassionate, culturally sensitive, and trauma-informed approach, I work with individuals, couples, and families. Whether you are dealing with anxiety, depression, relationship struggles, or the effects of past trauma, I am here to support you.
